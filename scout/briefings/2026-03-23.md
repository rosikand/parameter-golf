### 🔴 Priority 1: Validate and Stack 30-epoch Cosine TTT on the #401 Baseline
**Estimated impact: very high (potentially −0.05 to −0.10 BPB)**

PR #509 shows a claimed −10.8% BPB gain from extending TTT epochs 10→30 with cosine decay, but on a constrained 1×H100 run. The most valuable experiment right now is:
- Take the full #401 architecture (EMA+Tight SWA+Muon+VE128+LN Scale+Int6)
- Apply 30-epoch cosine TTT on top
- Run on standard 8×H100 with full training steps

This combo has **not been attempted** — #509 uses SwiGLU+U-Net without SWA/Muon, and #401 uses no TTT. The interaction between Tight SWA and long TTT is unknown but there's no obvious reason for conflict.

**Specific action:** Fork #401, add TTT with 30-epoch cosine schedule from #509, submit as record attempt.
